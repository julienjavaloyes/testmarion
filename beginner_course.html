French is not as difficult a language as you may think if you start learning the right way from the beginning

My years of teaching and my own experience showed me how the first month is critical when learning a new language

If you don't focus and start the wrong way you will end up:

  • Forgetting what you have just learned in a few days
  • Getting lost with the grammar
  • Developing bad habits (pronunciation, sentence structure, etc.) very difficult to break in the future

To avoid this, I have designed a programme specially conceived to start learning French with good bases:

  • Pronuncing the words correctly from the start
  • Acquiring the necessary vocabulary and grammar to use all the way during your learning process
  • Getting real results after one month and being able to have a short conversation with natives
  • Not giving up and staying motivated to continue learning French

How does it work?

  • 3h of conversation lessons and 3-5h self study between each lesson
  • Exclusive! After each lesson you receive the vocabulary with the recorded sound!
  • 450€ for the one-month programme (4 weeks)
