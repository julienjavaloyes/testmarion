My name is Marion and I'm a French certified teacher from the University of Strasbourg and an official DELF and TCF examiner.

After getting my master degree for teaching French, I have been teaching in France and abroad (Japan, Malaysia, Hong Kong) and online for more than 10 years.

I have helped students from many nationalities and backgrounds learn French for various purposes : expatriation to France or Canada, finding a job, improving daily life in a French-speaking environment or to pass an official exam.

Most of my students are still studying with me after 2, 3, or even 5 years ! More than just teaching, I believe that teachers can create an unique relationship with their students and help/guide them in many ways.

I'm passionate about teaching and creating educational resources and I love learning new languages: I can speak English and Japanese fluently and I'm learning more than four others! I know how difficult it is to learn a new language and I'm using all my experience in my classes to help my students as best as I can.

I'm looking forward to meeting you!
