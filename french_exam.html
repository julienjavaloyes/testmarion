Do you need to pass an official French exam (DELF/DALF/TCF/TEF)?

  • You want to apply for the 10-year resident card.
  • You want to apply for the French naturalization.
  • You want to assess your French level to find a job in France or in another French-speaking country.
  • You just want to take on the challenge or motivate yourself to study French.

Note that 2025 is the LAST year to apply for the 10-year resident card with an A2 level and for French naturalization with a B1 level.
Starting January 2026, you will need a B1 level for the 10-year resident card and a B2 level for naturalization. Don’t wait too long if you want to apply this year!

I have more than 10 years of experience teaching French and 5 years of experience training students to pass the official exams successfully.
I use textbooks and materials approved by the French government, and I organize my curriculum to help you succeed in the four parts of the exam.

Available Courses :

Preparation for the A2 and B1 official DELF exam with weekly group lessons and guided study.

What is included:

  • Extensive listening and reading practice exercises before each lesson
  • 5 weekly group lessons to practice speaking and to learn more about the exam content, with tips and advice
  • A weekly homework corrected by me to practice for the written part of the exam
  • 4 mock exams (listening, reading, and writing) corrected by me with a grade
  • 1 individual practice session for the speaking part of the exam (30 minutes) the last week of the programme.

When?

B1 : Fridays from 10 AM to 11 AM (Paris time) OR Saturdays from 11AM to 12PM (Paris time)

I also offer the same program without the group lessons and at a cheaper price:

  • You will receive the listening and reading exercises with corrections.
  • You will get my personal correction of a written exercise once a week, with advice and remarks to improve.
  • You can practice for the full exam with a corrected and graded mock exam (4 times for listening/reading and writing, 1 time for speaking with an individual session).

Total price: 80€ for 5 weeks + 1 individual speaking test session (30 minutes)

Conditions to register for the A2 or B1 exam preparation :

  • The program is non-refundable and must be paid in full before the classes start.
  • It is not possible to cancel or reschedule any lesson, but missed lessons will be recorded and shared.
  • You must already be at A2 or B1 level to join. The program is designed to help you prepare for the exams, not to advance to the next level or to review the grammar.
  • The fees to pass the DELF exam are not included in the program.
